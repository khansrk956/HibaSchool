Examination Hall Rules

No Cheating

Students are strictly prohibited from cheating or attempting to cheat in any form during the exam. This includes copying from others, using unauthorized materials, electronic devices, or communication with others.

No Talking

Students should maintain silence during the examination to avoid disturbing others and to focus on their own work.

No Leaving

Students are not allowed to leave the examination hall until they have completed their exam and handed in their answer sheets. Any student leaving early must do so quietly without disturbing others.

No Borrowing or Sharing

Students should not borrow or share stationery, calculators, or any other materials during the exam. Each student must bring their own necessary items.

No Unauthorized Items

Students are not allowed to bring any unauthorized items into the examination hall, including but not limited to electronic devices, books, notes, and bags.

No Communication

Students should not communicate with each other or pass any information during the exam. Any form of communication, verbal or non-verbal, is strictly prohibited.

No Disturbance

Students should refrain from any behavior that may disturb others, such as tapping pens, tapping feet, or making unnecessary movements.

Follow Instructions

Students must follow all instructions given by the invigilators or exam proctors. This includes starting and stopping writing at the specified times, and following any special instructions for the exam.

No Unauthorized Leaving

Once the examination has started, students are not allowed to leave the examination hall without permission from the invigilator.

Maintain Integrity

Students are expected to maintain the integrity of the examination process and to uphold academic honesty at all times.
